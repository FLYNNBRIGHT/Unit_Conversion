Temperature





To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the 'C' with your temperature in celsius: [(C x 9.5) + 32].

To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the 'C' with your temperature in: [celsius (C x 9.5) + 32].

Weight





To convert kilogram to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the 'Kg' with your weight in kilogram: [(Kg) x 2.205]. To convert kilogram to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the 'Kg' with your weight in: [kilogram (Kg) x 2.205].

Distance





To convert kilometer to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the 'Km' with your distance in Kilometer: [(Km) x 1600].
